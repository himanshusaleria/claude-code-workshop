# Ad Campaign Brief — Brain Dump

## Campaign: Infurnia Q2 Launch Push

### What are we promoting?
Infurnia's cloud-based interior design platform. Key selling points:
- Design in 2D AND 3D in one tool (no more switching between AutoCAD and SketchUp)
- Real-time collaboration (Google Docs for floor plans)
- Auto-generated BOQ (bill of quantities) — saves 2-3 days per project
- Client 3D walkthrough — clients can explore designs in 3D, leave comments
- Free trial available, no credit card needed

### Target Audience
- Interior designers (individual and studio heads)
- Design firm owners / creative directors
- Architecture firms with interior design divisions
- Age: 25-45
- Location: India (Tier 1 + Tier 2 cities)
- Interests: AutoCAD, SketchUp, interior design, architecture, design tools, home decor

### Budget
₹50,000 for 2 weeks. Split across 3-4 ad sets.

### Angles I'm thinking about

**Angle 1 — Pain of multiple tools**
"Tired of juggling AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Excel? What if one tool did it all?"
Target: Designers frustrated with their current workflow

**Angle 2 — Time saving (BOQ)**
"Your team spends 2-3 days on BOQ per project. What if it took 2 minutes?"
Target: Design firm owners / managers who care about efficiency

**Angle 3 — Client wow factor**
"Let your clients walk through their home in 3D before a single wall is built"
Target: Client-facing designers who want to impress

**Angle 4 — Collaboration**
"Your design team shouldn't be emailing DWG files back and forth. There's a better way."
Target: Teams of 5+ designers

### Offers
- 14-day free trial (no credit card)
- Free onboarding call for teams of 5+
- Special pricing for the first 50 sign-ups this quarter

### Creative Notes
- Use before/after format: messy workflow vs clean Infurnia workflow
- Show the actual product UI — the 3D visualization is impressive
- Testimonial from existing customer if possible
- Video ads (30 sec) + static image ads (1080x1080 and 1080x1920)

### What I need from this campaign
- 3-4 ad copy variations per angle (headline + primary text + description)
- Creative briefs for the designer
- Audience targeting suggestions for each angle
- A/B testing plan: which angles to test first
